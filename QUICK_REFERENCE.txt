================================================================================
QUICK REFERENCE: 5 NEW DYNAMIC FEATURES
================================================================================

FEATURE 1: SMS/WhatsApp Alerts
-------------------------------
POST /api/alerts/send              - Send alert
GET  /api/alerts/history           - View history
POST /api/alerts/auto-generate     - Auto-create alerts

FEATURE 2: Community Marketplace
---------------------------------
GET  /api/marketplace/listings     - Browse listings
POST /api/marketplace/create       - Create listing
GET  /api/marketplace/my-listings  - My listings
POST /api/marketplace/close/:id    - Close listing

FEATURE 3: Disease Detection
-----------------------------
POST /api/disease/detect           - Upload & detect
GET  /api/disease/history          - Detection history
POST /api/disease/resolve/:id      - Mark resolved

FEATURE 4: Loan & Insurance
----------------------------
GET  /api/loan/eligibility         - Check eligibility
POST /api/loan/apply               - Apply for loan
GET  /api/loan/history             - Loan history
GET  /api/insurance/plans          - View plans
POST /api/insurance/apply          - Apply insurance
GET  /api/insurance/my-policies    - My policies

FEATURE 5: Video Learning
--------------------------
GET  /api/videos/library           - Browse videos
POST /api/videos/watch/:id         - Watch video
POST /api/videos/progress/:id      - Update progress
GET  /api/videos/my-progress       - Watch history
POST /api/videos/like/:id          - Like video
